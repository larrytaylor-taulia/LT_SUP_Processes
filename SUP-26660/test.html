Using Taulia Q&A

Q. What is Taulia?

  • Taulia, a subsidiary of SAP, provides working capital solutions and a platform that allows Hapag-Lloyd suppliers to view the status of their invoices, receive remittance details, and request early payment for approved invoices. Taulia was acquired by SAP, March 10th, 2022, and is an official part of SAP’s solution portfolio. All accounts payable processes will continue to be owned by Hapag-Lloyd.

Q. Who should I invite to my portal account?

  • Please add others to your company who will need access to the portal including for checking invoices, payments, etc. Click here to learn how to add users to your account.

 

Please be informed that the maintenance of your Taulia account is fully under your responsibility.

Make sure that in the event of any change (users leaving your company, access removal, new admins, split/merge of companies, etc) your designated Taulia account administrator manages the complete access in the User Manager, by activating or deactivating users appropriately.

If you have only one admin user on the account, please be sure to add a new admin user before you leave the company. This will ensure that your company does not lose access to your Taulia portal.

Why is Hapag Lloyd offering this service to its suppliers?

Hapag Lloyd is introducing the Taulia portal to improve its supplier’s liquidity options, by giving them the opportunity to be paid upon approval of their invoices.

Q. What is the advantages of registering on the Taulia portal?

  • Early payment on your approved invoices – Once an invoice is approved, you can select it for early payment, rather than waiting for payment on the maturity date.
  • Increase visibility of your invoices – The Taulia portal will give you real time visibility of your invoices and payments.
  • Improve your working capital – Increasing your cashflow when you need it. It is under your control, once an invoice is approved, and eligible for early payment.
  • Alternative source of finance – the Taulia program allows you to be paid early to help fund your business, potentially freeing up your other credit facilities.
  • Decrease DSO – The program gives you the opportunity to reduce your Days Sales Outstanding from to the invoice approval date.

Click here to learn how to view your invoice status and details. Use the Hapag-Lloyd supplier platform to monitor the status of your invoices and request early payment.

 

Q. Is the process for submitting my invoice to Hapag-Lloyd changing?

  • No. Please continue to submit your invoices to Hapag-Lloyd like you do today. Your invoice will appear in the Taulia platform once Hapag-Lloyd enters your invoice in their accounting system. They may have an "Approved", "In Process", "Rejected/Voided"," or "Paid" status.

Q. I have questions related to the details of my invoice. Who should I contact?

  • Please contact Hapag-Lloyd directly. Invoices that appear in the platform are direct reflections of the details entered by Hapag-Lloyd .

Q. How soon will I see my invoice in the portal after I submit them to Hapag-Lloyd?

  • Invoices will appear in the portal once they have been matched against receipts by Hapag-Lloyd . They may have an "Approved", "In Process", "Rejected/Voided"," or "Paid" status. Click here to learn what each invoice status mean.

Q. What if my invoice is voided/rejected, what should I do?

  • Please contact Hapag-Lloyd directly. Do not contact Taulia Support. Taulia does not approve/reject invoices.

Q. I can't find the invoice I submitted to Hapag-Lloyd. What should I do?

  • Please contact Hapag-Lloyd directly if you are unable to find an invoice you submitted for processing.

Early Payment & Discounting Q&A

Use the Taulia platform to accelerate the payment of your invoices through the early payment options.

Q. I have questions regarding the early payment rates for invoices. Who should I contact?

  • Please contact Taulia Support for any early payment related questions.

Q. What is the acceleration window for Early Payment on my Hapag Lloyd invoices?

  • Early payment on your invoices is available from the point of approval, up until 9 days before your contractual payment date.

Q. Why do some approved invoices do not have an early payment offer?

  • It is within Hapag-Lloyd discretion to offer early payment on any approved invoice. Please contact Hapag-Lloyd if you have question.

Q. My invoice is approved but I can't request/accept early payment. Why?

  • If your invoice is approved but you are still unable to request an early payment this will most likely be the result of missing the early payment window. 

The Taulia solution offers a Cashflow Functionality option that will automatically request early payment as soon as your invoice is approved so that you never miss the early payment window.
